Weather Tidbits is something we started to enhance our proactive education program. On occasion, a member may initiate a discussion on a topic of choice... maybe just bringing up a weather-related definition or concept, or discussing current weather in some part of the country. Open discussion is encouraged among all members. We are all teachers, and we are all students!
